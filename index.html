What the paper establishes

Four dimensions

Proposes GenAI Literacy, Curriculum/Learning Design, Teaching and Learning, and Assessment as core dimensions.

Dual perspective

Integrates teachers' own competencies with their capacity to empower students' critical GenAI engagement.

Three-tier progression

Articulates Basic, Intermediate, and Advanced developmental levels from awareness to creative co-creation.
